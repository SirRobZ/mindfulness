Five Facet Mindfulness

Observe

Your ability to stay present with perceptions, sensations, thoughts, or feelings, even when they are unpleasant or painful; not distracting yourself.

Describe

Your ability to clearly express your beliefs, opinions, emotions, and expectations.

Acting with Awareness

Your ability to focus on the task at hand and ignore distractions.

Nonjudgement

Your ability to take in your own experience without automatically judging it as good or bad, right or wrong, fair or unfair, etc.

Nonreactivity

Your ability to control yourself rather than letting emotions control you.
